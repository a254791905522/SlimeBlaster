Privacy Policy

Slime Blaster

Overview

Slime Blaster is an offline single-player mobile game. We designed the game to work without account registration, online matchmaking, analytics services, or advertising SDKs.

Information We Collect

Slime Blaster does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The game does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or payment data.

Local Data Storage

The game stores gameplay progress locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, star ratings, settings preferences, and run-related status needed for the single-player experience. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to external servers by the game.

Children's Privacy

Because Slime Blaster does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly gather personal data from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

Slime Blaster does not rely on third-party analytics, ad networks, or social login services for normal gameplay. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated before release.

Data Security

Since gameplay data is stored locally and not sent to our servers, your privacy exposure is limited to the security of your own device and platform environment.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy if the game’s data practices change. Any future update will be reflected on this page together with a revised effective date.
